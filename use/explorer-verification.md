# Verify Transactions and Assets with Explorer

Explorer is the entry point for ordinary users to verify transactions, assets, and contract state. It does not replace wallet authorization or Indexer APIs, but it makes on-chain evidence readable.

## What You Can Verify

1. Bitcoin L1 funding, splicing, close, and punish transactions.
2. SatoshiNet unlock, lock, anchor, and deAnchor transactions.
3. Whether a UTXO exists or is spent.
4. Asset amounts and protocol state.
5. Contract deployment, invocation, and Result TX.
6. Channel address, transaction history, and cross-layer evidence.
