Decentralized Data Storage
====

The future crypto ecosystem will have at least three tiers of storage:
1. Storage on the BTC mainnet: the most expensive, but also the most valuable.
2. Storage on BTC's extended network, such as SatoshiNet, is 100 times cheaper, but still relatively expensive.
3. Other specialized on-chain storage solutions are 100 times cheaper than SatoshiNet, and likely comparable in price to various current centralized network storage solutions.

A 100-fold price difference between each tier is required to create a relatively stable market.
