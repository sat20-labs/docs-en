# I Run a BTC Community

If you run a BTC asset community, inscription community, Runes / BRC20 / ORDX community, SatoshiNet aims to help you build and gradually operate your own financial infrastructure.

You do not need to understand every STP, Indexer, or contract detail first. Start by deciding what capabilities your community wants.

## What You Can Build

1. Your own SatoshiNet Core Node.
2. Your own L1 / L2 Indexer.
3. Your own Explorer.
4. Your own wallet entry or Wallet SDK integration.
5. Your own Launchpad.
6. Your own AMM pool.
7. Your own limit order market.
8. Your own DAO and community fund workflow.
9. Your own DEX frontend and backend.
10. Your own brand, domain, asset rules, and operating rules.

## Recommended Flow

1. Read [Community Stack](../community-stack/).
2. Check [SatoshiNet Today](../ecosystem/satoshinet-today.md).
3. Prepare a community requirements brief.
4. Submit cooperation intent through [Builder Program](../ecosystem/builder-program.md).
5. Deploy a community DEX / DAO prototype on testnet first.
6. Verify assets, transactions, and contract state with Explorer, Indexer, and wallet.
