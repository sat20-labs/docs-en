## Three-Minute Check

1. Are you serving asset flow, community governance, contract applications, infrastructure, or AI automation?
2. Do the asset facts you need come from Bitcoin L1, SatoshiNet L2, or cross-layer evidence between them?
3. Do your users need to retain their own asset exit paths?
4. Do you want to complete testnet validation, public showcase, or cooperation application first?

If your goal is to build full infrastructure for a BTC community, start with [Community Stack](../community-stack/).
